Let you are hired to create a software for a bank. For their first version you are required to store
account holder information where you need to store account holder name, national id, address, bank account id,
initial amount of money. Account holder can deposit money and withdraw money. But to withdraw money 
account holder needs to have more than 100 balance in his account. If the balance is more than 100000,
then bank will give him 2% interest of his main balance, else he will get 1% interest of his main balance.  

1. Now write necessary classes and methods.
2. Calculate monthly interest and yearly interest.
